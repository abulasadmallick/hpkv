About Our School

Hanspukuria Vidyapith (H.S.) has been a cornerstone of education in the Nadia district since its establishment in 1954. For over seven decades, we have been dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where students can achieve academic excellence and grow into responsible, well-rounded individuals.

Our History

Founded by a group of visionary community leaders, the school started with a humble mission: to make quality education accessible to the children of Hanspukuria and its surrounding villages. From a small building with a handful of students, we have grown into a higher secondary institution known for its commitment to quality teaching and strong ethical values.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To impart knowledge and skills that empower students to succeed in a competitive world, while instilling in them a sense of discipline, social responsibility, and respect for our cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for its academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and its role in shaping the future leaders of our society.
