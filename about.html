About Our School

Hanspukuria Vidyapith, established on 2nd February 1950, stands as a beacon of knowledge and enlightenment, fostering generations with its commitment to excellence in education. For over seven decades, it has been a nurturing ground where young minds are shaped into thoughtful, responsible citizens. With a legacy rooted in values and a vision for the future, Hanspukuria Vidyapith continues to inspire and empower, carrying forward its rich tradition of academic and cultural brilliance. Each passing year adds to its illustrious history, reminding us of its pivotal role in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

From the Head of the Institute

"Hanspukuria Vidyapith is known for its strong educational foundation and commitment to nurturing students. The school has built a reputation for providing quality education while focusing on the all-round development of its students. The teaching staff at Hanspukuria Vidyapith are dedicated and work diligently to foster both academic excellence and moral values in students. The school also emphasizes discipline, extracurricular activities, and community involvement, helping students grow into responsible and well-rounded individuals. Its contribution to local education in the region of Nadia is commendable, and it's a place where students are encouraged to strive for their best, not only in academics but also in sports and cultural activities.."

From the President of SMC

"Hanspukuria Vidyapith is a school that stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development. With a nurturing environment, it encourages students to reach their full potential, both intellectually and personally. The institution emphasizes the importance of education, discipline, and moral values, making it a respected place in the local community.The school’s dedication to providing a well-rounded education, including opportunities for extracurricular involvement, ensures that students not only excel in academics but also develop important life skills. It continues to shape young minds for a brighter future, instilling confidence and leadership in its students."
