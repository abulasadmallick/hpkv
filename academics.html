Academics

Our academic program is designed to be rigorous, engaging, and comprehensive, providing students with a strong foundation for higher education and future careers. We follow the curriculum prescribed by the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) and the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE).

Examination System

We conduct regular assessments to monitor student progress and prepare them for board examinations. Our examination system includes:

  • Unit Tests /Summative Assessments
  • Class Tests
  • Annual / Final Examinations
  • Semester Examinations for classes XI and XII

Performance reports are shared with parents and guardians regularly to ensure a collaborative approach to each student's academic journey.
