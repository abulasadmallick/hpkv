About School

Hanspukuria Vidyapith (H.S.), established on 2nd February 1950, began its remarkable journey in education with a commitment to excellence. Nestled in the serene village of Hanspukuria, the school enjoys a picturesque location beside the tranquil Jalangi River, offering a peaceful and inspiring environment for learning.


Over the past seven decades, the school has shaped the lives of countless talented individuals, leaving a lasting imprint on its history. Recognized as one of the finest institutions in West Bengal, it stands as a symbol of academic distinction and cultural heritage. Hanspukuria Vidyapith remains dedicated to honoring its glorious past, embracing its vibrant present, and aspiring toward a promising future.



Platinum Jubilee Celebration of Hanspukuria Vidyapith (H.S.)

Hanspukuria Vidyapith (H.S.) proudly announces its Platinum Jubilee Celebration, marking 75 glorious years of excellence in education and holistic development. Since its establishment, the school has been a beacon of knowledge, nurturing countless young minds and shaping their futures with dedication and commitment.


The Platinum Jubilee is not just a celebration of the past but also a moment to look forward to the future with renewed determination and aspirations. The event will feature a blend of cultural programs, alumni reunions, exhibitions, and discussions, showcasing the rich legacy and achievements of the institution.

We invite all current and former students, staff, and well-wishers to join us in this momentous occasion and make it a memorable chapter in the history of Hanspukuria Vidyapith. Stay tuned to our website for updates and details about the celebration schedule. Together, let us honor the past and stride confidently into the future!
