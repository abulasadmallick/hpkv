Admissions Information

We are delighted that you are considering Hanspukuria Vidyapith for your child's education. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. Please review the information below carefully.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission is open to all, subject to the availability of seats. The age eligibility for admission to different classes as of January 1st, 2026, is as follows:

Class Minimum Age Maximum Age
Class V 10+ Years 11 Years
Class VI 11+ Years 12 Years
Class XI (Science) 15+ Years -
Class XI (Arts) 15+ Years -

Admission Process

  1. Collect Application Form: Application forms will be available at the school office from the first week of December.
  2. Submit Form: Submit the filled-in application form along with the required documents at the school office.
  3. Admission Test: An admission test will be conducted for prospective students. The date and time will be communicated upon form submission.
  4. Merit List Publication: A merit list of selected candidates will be published on the school notice board and website.
  5. Final Admission: Selected candidates must complete all admission formalities, including fee payment, within the stipulated dates.

Required Documents

Please submit self-attested photocopies of the following documents along with the application form:

  • Two recent passport-size photographs of the student.
  • Copy of the student's Birth Certificate.
  • Copy of the student's Aadhar Card.
  • Previous year's report card / mark sheet.
  • Family income certificate (if applicable).
